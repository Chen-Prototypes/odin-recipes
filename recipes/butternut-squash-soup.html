Sweet and Spicy Butternut Squash Soup

Description

This rich, velvety soup blends the sweet, nutty flavor of butternut squash with a kick of spice. Perfect for a chilly evening! Inspired from Guild Wars 2.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Pumpkin seeds or fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion, and sauté until it becomes translucent and starts to brown, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and ginger to the pot and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the cubed butternut squash to the pot. Sprinkle over the chili powder, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine and cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the butternut squash is tender and can be easily pierced with a fork.
  5. Once the butternut squash is cooked, blend the soup until smooth. You can use an immersion blender directly in the pot, or transfer the soup to a counter top blender (in batches, if necessary) and then return it to the pot.
  6. Stir in the coconut milk until well combined. If necessary, you can adjust the seasoning at this point, adding more salt, pepper, or spices to taste.
  7. Ladle the soup into bowls. If desired, garnish with a swirl of extra coconut milk, a sprinkling of pumpkin seeds, or some fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy your sweet and spicy butternut squash soup, inspired by Guild Wars 2!
