Drake Kabob

Description

A savory and hearty meal straight from the Guild Wars universe. This Drake Kabob recipe is a delicious fusion of exotic flavors, bringing the fantasy to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken or pork (substitute for "Drake Meat")
  • 2 bell peppers (variety of colors)
  • 1 large red onion
  • 1 large zucchini
  • 1 cup of pineapple chunks
  • For the marinade:
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the marinade: In a medium bowl, mix together the olive oil, soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, ground ginger, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cut the meat into 1-inch pieces. Place the meat into the bowl with the marinade. Mix well until all the meat is coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. Cut the bell peppers, red onion, and zucchini into 1-inch chunks.
  4. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Assemble the kabobs: Thread the marinated meat, bell peppers, onion, zucchini, and pineapple chunks onto skewers, alternating as you go.
  6. Place the kabobs onto the grill. Cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until the meat is fully cooked and vegetables are tender.
  7. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your Guild Wars Drake Kabobs!
